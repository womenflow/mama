Staying active during pregnancy offers many benefits for both mother and baby. Movement helps maintain fitness, improves mood, and can ease labor.

**Benefits of Prenatal Exercise**

Regular activity strengthens muscles needed for labor and recovery. It improves circulation, helps manage weight gain, and reduces pregnancy discomfort like back pain.

**Safe Exercise Options**

Walking is the safest option for most pregnancies. Swimming provides excellent low-impact movement. Prenatal yoga focuses on gentle stretching and breathing.

**Important Precautions**

Always consult your healthcare provider before starting exercise. Avoid high-impact activities and overheating. Stop if you feel dizzy or experience pain.

**Listening to Your Body**

Your body changes throughout pregnancy. What feels comfortable in the first trimester may need modification later. Honor these changes and adjust accordingly.

**Community and Support**

Join prenatal exercise classes to connect with other expecting mothers. Professional guidance ensures your workouts remain safe and effective.
